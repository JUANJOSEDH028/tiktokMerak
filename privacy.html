Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • TikTok Account Information: Username, display name, and profile picture
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth access tokens (stored securely and encrypted)
  • Content Metadata: Video titles, descriptions, and hashtags you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Publish video content on your behalf
  • Display your profile information in our dashboard
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All authentication tokens are encrypted at rest
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • We never store your TikTok password
  • Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With TikTok, as necessary to provide our service
  • When required by law or legal process

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Revoke our application's access at any time through your TikTok account settings
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Update or correct any information we have

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our service. When you revoke access or request deletion, we remove your data within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with TikTok's official API. Please review TikTok's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or through our service.
